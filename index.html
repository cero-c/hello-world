Why am I here?

Greta Thunberg's speech at the United Nations in 2019 wasn't just powerful—it was a wake-up call. At just 16, she showed the world that one voice can spark change, no matter how small it seems. Her courage reminded us that even the simplest actions, when taken seriously, can make a big difference for our planet. Inspired by her resilience, I created this website to share small, practical ways you can protect our blue home. From tiny daily habits to smarter choices, these tips are here to help you make a positive impact—because just like Greta showed, change starts with each of us daring to act.

Why are you here?

You're here because you care—about the planet, your future, and the small steps that can lead to big change. Whether you're looking to live more sustainably, reduce waste, or simply make mindful choices, this is the place to start transforming your lifestyle for a healthier Earth. Let's take the first step together!
